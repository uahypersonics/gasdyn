# Prandtl-Meyer Expansion

Prandtl-Meyer theory describes smooth, isentropic turning of supersonic flow through an expansion fan.

## Prandtl-Meyer Function

For Mach number $M$ and ratio of specific heats $\gamma$:

$$
\nu(M) = \sqrt{\frac{\gamma+1}{\gamma-1}}\,\tan^{-1}\!\left(\sqrt{\frac{\gamma-1}{\gamma+1}(M^2-1)}\right) - \tan^{-1}\!\left(\sqrt{M^2-1}\right)
$$

Across an isentropic expansion:

$$
\nu_2 = \nu_1 + \theta
$$

where $\theta$ is the flow turning angle.
